Investment Thesis

We invest at the
frontier of science
and technology.

Jolt backs exceptional founders at the intersection of deep science and transformative technology — teams who are building platforms, not just products, in markets that don't yet fully exist.

We move fast, write conviction-led checks, and bring operational depth from our own experience as founders and scientists. Beyond capital, we open doors to strategic partners, CDMOs, pharma, and follow-on investors who move the needle.

Our check sizes range from pre-seed through Series A, targeting companies where scientific de-risking is the primary value driver and where a founder's technical edge is the moat.
